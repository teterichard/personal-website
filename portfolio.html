Smart traffic light

In this image down below, you can see a smart traffic light project, that may look a complete mess, but believe me, it makes sense. I develop this project during my IOT class together with my two team mates (Maria Antonia and Luiz Gustavo) and it was an incredible experience!

Well, let me explain a little bit of our project... A smart traffic light is a system that combines convetional traffic lights with sensors, which presents a wide variety. It is by those that the system is informed about the vehicle flow and regulates the time and which traffic light should be open or closed. In our case, we dicided to use a LDR (Light Dependent Resistor), which would be installed on asphalt to control the vehicle flow. (You can see the 3 LDR we used on the bottom right corner). We planned our traffic light to be implemented at the intersection of two lanes, making the time of green light increase whenever the vehicle flow is more volumed.

Rocket made from a Pet bottle

My friends from school, Maria Laura and Beatriz, and I built this rocket made of PET bottle to compete in MOBFOG (Mostra Brasileira de Foguetes - "Brazilian Rocket Show"), which makes part of OBA (Olimpíada Brasileira de Astronomia e Astronáutica - "Brazilian Astronomy and Astronautics Olympiad"). The substances we used as fuel were bicarbonate and vinegar and our rocket achieved 180 meters of reach.

Oxfordmun 2020: women in STEM

Oxford MUN was the first simulation in my life, and it was really challenging and exciting. At the simulation, I had the opportunity to discuss and fight for Women in STEM, and in some way fight for my own future. To write my position paper, I searched for different realities than mine, since it was a global simulation and I was representing the Republic of Korea, a country I knew almost nothing about. In 2021, because I liked ROK so much, I actually took Korean classes, which, I have to admit, are very different from what I am used to, since they have a completely different alphabet, and it was too much for me:(. It was empowering to overcome a little bit of my difficulties and shyness! It also was special to me since I had connections with girls from all over the world, with different points of view, cultures, and every single one was special in their own way, and established a bond, since we all had the same passion and were passing through a difficult moment with the pandemic.
